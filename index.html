🌍 Heart of the Congo

An Interactive Journey into Darkness and Discovery

Welcome, Traveler

In the autumn of 1890, the Company offers you a contract. The pay is extraordinary, the prospects uncertain. You will venture into the heart of Africa, where civilization fades into shadow and every choice carries weight.

Heart of the Congo is an interactive narrative experience inspired by Joseph Conrad's timeless novella. Your decisions will shape the story, determining whether you emerge enlightened, corrupted, or lost in the darkness.

The Five Phases

I. The Contract

In Brussels, you meet the Company. Will you accept their doctrine, or challenge the very foundations of their enterprise?

II. The Coast

You arrive at a desolate Station. The Grove of Death awaits. Trust the Manager, or venture where shadows creep?

III. The River

Upstream lies the Inner Station and a man named Kurtz. Will you continue toward the unknown, or turn back?

IV. The Inner Station

At last, you meet Kurtz—genius, god, or madman? Will you save him or leave him to his fate?

Coda: The Return

The river carries you home. But you are changed. What truth will you carry back to civilization?

Key Themes

🔀 Choice Matters: Every decision you make influences your character's morality and ultimate fate. There are no "right" answers, only consequences.
🌑 Darkness & Light: Navigate the tension between civilization and savagery, between personal ambition and human compassion.
💎 Multiple Endings: Will you emerge as a witness to truth, a disillusioned wanderer, lost in the wilderness, complicit in evil, or claimed by the jungle itself?
📖 Interactive Narrative: Experience a story that adapts to your choices, creating a unique journey each time you play.
